# Onboarding Guide

## Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Visit [signup page](https://app.example.com/signup).
2. Enter your name, work email, and a strong password.
3. Verify your email address via the confirmation link.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Profile
- Add your organization name and logo in Account > Profile.
- Configure your timezone and language preferences.

## Step 3: Invite Your Team
- Go to Account > Team > Invite Members.
- Enter email addresses and assign roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer).
- Team members will receive an invitation email.

## Step 4: Connect Integrations
- Navigate to Settings > Integrations.
- Available integrations: Slack, Jira, GitHub, Salesforce, Zapier.
- Follow the on-screen OAuth flow to authorize each integration.

## Step 5: Import Your Data
- Go to Settings > Data > Import.
- Supported formats: CSV, JSON, Excel.
- Use the provided import template to ensure correct formatting.

## Step 6: Explore the Dashboard
- The Home dashboard provides an overview of key metrics.
- Use the left navigation to access all features.
- Pin frequently used views using the ★ icon.

## Getting Help
- In-app help: Click the "?" icon in the bottom-right corner.
- Documentation: [docs.example.com](https://docs.example.com)
- Support: support@example.com or live chat (Business hours: Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm UTC)

## Onboarding Checklist
- [ ] Email verified
- [ ] Profile completed
- [ ] Team invited
- [ ] At least one integration connected
- [ ] First data import completed
