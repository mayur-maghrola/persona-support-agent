# Payment & Refund Policy

## Payment Processing
- All payments are processed securely via Stripe.
- Accepted payment methods: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, Bank Transfer (Enterprise only).
- Charges appear on your statement as "EXAMPLE INC".

## Refund Eligibility

### Monthly Plans
- Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing period has started.

### Annual Plans
- Full refund available within 7 days of purchase if the service has not been substantially used.
- Prorated refund available within 30 days for unused months.
- No refunds after 30 days.

### Add-ons & One-Time Charges
- Refundable within 48 hours if unused.
- Contact support with proof of non-use for review.

## How to Request a Refund
1. Go to Account > Billing > Refund Request, or
2. Email billing@support.example.com with:
   - Account email
   - Invoice number
   - Reason for refund request

Refund requests are reviewed within 3–5 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

## Chargebacks
- Filing a chargeback without contacting support first may result in account suspension.
- Please reach out to billing@support.example.com before initiating a chargeback.

## Failed Payments
- If a payment fails, we retry up to 3 times over 7 days.
- After 3 failed attempts, the subscription is suspended until payment is updated.
- Update your payment method at Account > Billing > Payment Methods.
