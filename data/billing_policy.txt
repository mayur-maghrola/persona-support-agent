BILLING POLICY

Billing Cycle
- Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually depending on the selected plan.
- Billing date is set on the day of initial subscription activation.

Payment Methods
- Accepted: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal
- Payments are processed securely via Stripe

Late Payments
- A grace period of 7 days is provided after a missed payment.
- Services may be suspended after the grace period expires.
- A late fee of $5 or 2% of the outstanding amount (whichever is greater) may apply.

Invoices
- Invoices are emailed automatically on each billing date.
- Past invoices are available in Account > Billing > Invoice History.

Plan Changes
- Upgrades take effect immediately; charges are prorated.
- Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Taxes
- Applicable taxes (e.g., VAT, GST) are added based on your billing address.
- Tax-exempt customers must submit a valid exemption certificate.

Disputes
- Billing disputes must be submitted within 30 days of the charge.
- Contact billing@support.example.com with your invoice number and issue description.
