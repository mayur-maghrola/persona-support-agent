# Login Issues

## Cannot Log In

### Wrong Password
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset your password.
- Ensure Caps Lock is not enabled.

### Account Locked
- Accounts lock after 5 failed attempts.
- Wait 15 minutes for an automatic unlock or contact support for immediate assistance.

### Account Not Activated
- Check your email for an activation link sent at registration.
- Request a new activation email from the login page.

## SSO / OAuth Issues
- Ensure your SSO provider (Google, Microsoft, Okta) session is active.
- Clear browser cookies and retry.
- Verify that your organization's SSO configuration is correctly set up in the Admin Panel.

## MFA Problems
- Ensure your authenticator app clock is synced.
- Use a backup code if you've lost access to your MFA device.
- Contact support to disable MFA if you are completely locked out.

## Browser Issues
- Clear cache and cookies.
- Disable browser extensions that may block scripts.
- Try an incognito/private window or a different browser.

## Still Cannot Log In?
Contact support at support@example.com with:
- Your account email
- A description of the error message
- Browser and OS details
