SUBSCRIPTION FAQ

Q: What plans are available?
A: We offer three plans — Starter, Professional, and Enterprise. Visit our pricing page for details.

Q: Can I switch plans?
A: Yes. Upgrades are immediate and prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, a 14-day free trial is available for the Professional plan. No credit card required.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Account > Subscription > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Q: Will I get a refund if I cancel early?
A: Monthly plans are non-refundable. Annual plans may qualify for a prorated refund within 30 days of purchase.

Q: Can I pause my subscription?
A: Pausing is available for Professional and Enterprise plans. Contact support to request a pause.

Q: How many users are included?
A: Starter: 1 user. Professional: up to 10 users. Enterprise: unlimited users.

Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?
A: Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. You can export it from Account > Data > Export.

Q: Can I get an annual invoice?
A: Yes. Switch to annual billing in Account > Billing to receive a single annual invoice.

Q: How do I add more users?
A: Go to Account > Team > Invite Members. Additional user seats may incur extra charges on some plans.
